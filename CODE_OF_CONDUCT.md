# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Pocket PDF to be a welcoming and respectful project for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving useful feedback
- Being patient with contributors at different skill levels
- Focusing on the product and code, not the person

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Deliberately hostile or disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, contributions, or participants that violate this code of conduct.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in project discussions, issues, pull requests, and other community spaces related to Pocket PDF.
